About Us

The Moore Middle School Mathletes Club is a place for students to explore their passion for mathematics in a fun and engaging environment. We work on challenging problems, prepare for competitions, and discover the beauty of numbers and logic. Our goal is to build critical thinking skills and show that math is not just about memorization, but about creativity and problem-solving.

About the AMC 8

The AMC 8 is a 25-question, 40-minute, multiple-choice competition for middle school students, focusing on problem-solving skills. It's a great opportunity to test your math abilities against students from around the country. Our club helps members prepare with practice problems and strategies for success.

What is it? A nationally recognized math competition.

When is it? In January this year.

How do we prepare? We dedicate several meetings to working through past AMC 8 problems and exploring relevant topics.
